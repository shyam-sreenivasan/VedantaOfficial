Reading Club

Age: 8+ years
Size : 10 students

Meets every Sunday @ 11.15AM - 11.30AM (15 Minutes)

Place: VVP

Agenda:

* Commit the goal for how many words to be read for next Week
* Submit the actual words read in the current week.
* Q-A on the book read.
* Honor the Weekly Winners.
* Review the monthly standings on Words Read in that month.
* Submit and get feedback on a write-up of the story.

Fee per Session*: $1 for Members, $5 for all others
