  • listening skills improve when they follow a story through narration
  • attention span and focus increases when they listen to stories.
  • imaginative skills develop when they tread along several turning points in the story as they visualize the scenes and try to picturize the words they hear
  • devotion towards God can easily be planted in young minds through stories.
