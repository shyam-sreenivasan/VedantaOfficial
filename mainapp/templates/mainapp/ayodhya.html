WHY RAMAYANA?

  • listening skills improve when your child follows a story through narration
  • your child's attention span and focus increases through listening.
  • imaginative skills develop in a child when following along several turning points in the story and visualizing the scenes and trying to put pictures for words
  • devotion towards God can easily be planted in young minds through stories.

Valmiki's Ramayana is one the greatest epics of all time teaching morals and values to humanity. Rama, the hero of Ramayana, the incarnation of Vishnu, had shown how to live a quality life practicing dharmic principles while being brave and smart. There can be no other better resource to teach behavior and conduct to children than through the story of Ramayana as shown by Rama and Sita.
