About Us


Vedanta Peetam was established in 2007 by Sri Parthaji to teach Sanskrit slokas to children in the Sunnyvale neighborhood in California, US. Since then it has grown into an institution teaching not just slokas but imparting ancient wisdom through stories and discourses. Tens of hundreds of students have benefited in the last few years through passionate and dedicated classes conducted on a weekly basis at the Vedanta Peetam residence in Sunnyvale.
