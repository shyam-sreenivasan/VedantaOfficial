About Us


Established in 2007, Vedanta Vidya Peetam has been pivotal in reinstating the legacies of slokas, moral values and social services through hindu culture and tradition catering the needs of different ages. Story time, an enthusiatic and ebullient session,is popular among kids which tries to instill supreme moral values through stories from ancient hindu scriptures. Clubs and cultural activities, the other services offered, help in the overall personality development of individuals. Most of the proceeds are spent towards charitable causes. The organisation is dedicated to promote bhakti gnana and vairaghya and teach the invaluable ancient values in a modern way.


The Team

Parthasarathy Venkatavaradhan (Founder)

Parthasarathy has been training students in slokas since 2007 and has extensive experience in recitation, trodding the footsteps of his father and guru Sri Venkatavaradhan and his second guru Sri Sunder Kidambi of www.prapatti.com. He has a vision to teach as many children as possible and educate them about the ancient values with complete blessings from His Holiness,Shri Ranganatha Yathisekarar,the 46th Jeer of Ahobila Mutt.

Ramaa Parthasarathy (President)

Sharing similar visions with the founder, the president, Mrs. Ramaa is a relentless social worker who acknowledges ancient values and strives to see a better world of peace and glory through the eyes of Vedanta Vidya Peetam.

Bhuvana Iyer (Music Teacher)

Bhuvana Iyer is a dedicated teacher with over 20 years of training in Indian classical vocal music under renowned gurus in India such as Padma Bhushan Sangeeta Kala Acharya Sri.P. S. Narayanaswamy, Sangeeta Kala Acharya Smt. Seethalakshmi Venkatesan and Vidushi Smt. Suguna Purushothaman. Bhuvana has also received training and guidance in Hindustani classical and semi-classical styles in Mumbai and has won several awards in inter-school and inter-collegiate competitions.

Dr.R.Thirunarayanan (Advisor)

With over 40 years of experience in teaching sanskrit, Dr.R.Thirunarayanan , a recipient of the Shaastra Cuudaamani Award from the Govt. Of India has been the advisor and a well wisher of Vedanta Vidya Peetam. He is well known for his lectures in English, Tamil and Sanskrit in many Universities in India and US on profound topics like thiruppaavai, swami deshika stotras, etc. He also conducts sanskrit classes through webinars, Skype, Voneage phone and Google Hangounts.

Swamy Krishnamacharya (Advisor)

Swamy Krishnamacharya has over 42 years of serving Sriman Narayana. He has been with Malibu Temple, LA for the last 30+ years. Before this, Swamy had been serving in Thirumala Thirupathy Devasthanam as a Priest. Swamy also served at Balaji Temple in Australia. Swamy Krishnamacharya has extensive experience in Vedas, Agamas, Sutras and Grantha. Swamy Krishnamacharya has been providing invaluable advise to Vedanta Vidya Peetam in its operations for the last one year.

SERVICES


SLOKAS

From simple two liners to complex verses, slokas are taught to different age groups under a reward based system. Slokas improve memory, lung capacity, concentration and focus, vocal strength and great clarity in pronunciations. Offered at different levels from beginner to expeert, slokas are one of the ancient heritages to be embraced.

MUSIC

The tanpura is set to the optimum scale. The musician aligns his mind body and consciouness to the subtle frequencies of nature, unleashing the mathematical art of sound which strikes the listener's ears and makes him wonder, "What more do I need, I have found the ambrosia of existence!". Yes, the Indian Classical Music, the ancient, the root, is highly revered at Vedanta Vidya Peetam and offered under different streams from Carnatic vocals to percussions like Mridangam, including other classicals instruments like Veena and Violin.

VALUE EDUCATION

The importance of value education is indispensable. With the advent of the ever advancing technology,the ability to socialize and create real healthy relationships have declined. The moral values have plummeted too. It is vital for the society to bring back the invaluable moral values to the younger generation. We at Vedanta Vidya Peetam aim to instill such high moral values through the stories and practices prescribed in the ancient Hindu texts,epics and scriptures



ATHLETIC CLUB

Switch off your Ipads and TV's. Get on the field for some exciting and fun activities like hiking, jogging, cycling , games and marathons. Yes, Vedanta Vidya Peetam is all set to launch the Athletic club this summer conducting various athletic events and competitions. The winners will be rewarded for their excellence and athletic feats. Certificates will also be issued recognizing the active participation of children and motivating them to take up more physical activities and sports.

YOGA (Coming Soon)

The science of Yoga needs no introduction. Yoga is not about complex interlacing postures. It is the science of the mind, body and the soul and the art of aligning all the three into one concrete supreme bolt of energy. Yoga is revered for the health benefits, provided one practices meticulously. We at Vedanta Vidya Peetam acknowledge this ancient treasure and we strive to reinforce the yogic practices into the modern lifestyle for a healthy, stress-free and a happy life

BEES (Coming Soon)

Do you want to know what it feels like to be a BEE? Do you want to become an expert in math, science, geography, etc and ace the school level BEE's? Fasten your seat belts, as Vedanta Vidya Peetam is here to provide you with master class sessions for you to nail the BEE's and Olympiads.
